Organic Milk A2/A2

$5.99/64 Fl Oz
Organic
Kosher

The Whole Milk profile means richer, creamier flavor that pairs so well with cake, cookies, cereal, coffee, and more. And these traits can even re-invigorate that nostalgic idea of enjoying a tall, cold glass of milk! To support all the Whole Milk lovers, we offer a variety of options for different tastes and preferences, and now were adding one more variety to our dairy deck: Trader Joes Organic A2 Whole Milk.

What is A2? Glad you asked. Regular milk contains two variations of beta-casein protein: A1 and A2. Several years back, super-smart scientists in New Zealand discovered that some cows were producing milk with only A2 beta-casein. This A2 milk didnt have a different flavor. It didnt even have a different nutrient profile. What it did have going for it, different than regular milk, was the prospect of being easier to digest for some people. These scientists realized that you could easily discover which cows were producing A2-only milk using a simple genetic test. The rest is historyincluding the advent of A2 Whole Milk in the dairy case at your neighborhood Trader Joes!
